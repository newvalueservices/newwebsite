About This Project

This guest bathroom got a fresh, modern makeover from top to bottom.

We wrapped the walk-in shower in soft marble-look tile behind frameless glass, set a crisp white shaker vanity with a quartz top, and finished it all with matte black fixtures and warm bulb lighting.

The result is a calm, hotel-style space — easy to clean, bright, and a pleasure for guests to use.
