About This Project

This Allen master bath had all the classic early-2000s hallmarks — a bulky corner jacuzzi that nobody used, dark wood cabinets, and beige tile covering every surface from floor to ceiling. The homeowners wanted something bright, open, and timeless.

We removed the jacuzzi entirely and used that footprint to build a large frameless glass shower with marble-look tile. A new gray double vanity replaced the old dark cabinets, and backlit LED mirrors above each sink brought the whole space to life.

Warm wood-look flooring replaced the dated beige tile, tying everything together. Same footprint — completely different feel. Bright, open, and spa-worthy.
