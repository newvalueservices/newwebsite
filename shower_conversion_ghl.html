About This Project

This McKinney homeowner had a standard tub/shower combo that hadn't been used in years. They wanted something they'd actually look forward to every morning — a clean, open walk-in shower in the same footprint.

We removed the old tub and dated beige tile surround, waterproofed the alcove, and built a custom walk-in shower in its place. The new shower is tiled floor-to-ceiling in large-format gray stone-look porcelain, with a built-in tile niche for storage and a glass shower door that opens the space right up.

A square rain shower head with matte black controls ties the whole look together. Completed in one week — same footprint, completely transformed bathroom.
